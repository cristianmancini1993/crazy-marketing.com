About us

Crazy Marketing is the brand of EASY PEASY GROUP LIMITED — an Italian company that brings carefully selected, useful products to your home.

Our story

EASY PEASY GROUP LIMITED was founded in Rovereto, in the Trentino region of northern Italy, with one simple idea: to make practical, high-quality products for the home, garden, and everyday life accessible to everyone — without going through massive marketplaces, and without forcing customers to pay before they see what they're buying.

That's how crazy-marketing.com was born: a curated showcase of items we test ourselves before listing them. No useless gadgets, no products picked for margin alone — every item on Crazy Marketing solves a real problem.

What we do

  • We curate practical products for home and garden, sourced directly from certified European suppliers.
  • We test every item before listing it: if it doesn't convince us, we don't sell it.
  • We ship within 24-48 hours across 18 European countries with the most reliable couriers.
  • We guarantee cash on delivery: you only pay the courier — in cash — once the package is in your hands.
  • We support our customers with a native-language call center, 6 days a week.

Our values

  • Total transparency — No hidden costs, no surprises. The price you see is the price you pay, with shipping always included.
  • Customer-first — If you're not satisfied, you have 30 days to return the product for a full refund. No questions, no waiting.
  • Quality before price — We work with certified European suppliers. Our products comply with EU safety and environmental regulations.
  • Local service — Our customer service teams speak your language and respond within 1 hour on weekdays.

By the numbers

  • Over 3,842 verified customer reviews
  • 4.8 / 5 average rating
  • 18 European countries served with 24-48h delivery
  • 30-day refund guaranteed (beyond the 14 days required by EU law)
  • 24-month legal warranty on every product
